# Gumroad Pipeline

## Context

Use this skill to create, launch, and monetize a digital product on Gumroad. Covers ideation through post-launch optimization including email funnels, sales pages, and upsell sequences.

**Trigger phrases:** "launch a Gumroad product," "digital product funnel," "Gumroad sales page," "email nurture sequence."

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Ideation & Validation

1. **Define audience:** role, pain point, desired transformation, online communities.
2. **Choose format by effort-to-value:**
   - Fast: Notion templates, cheat sheets, swipe files
   - Medium: eBooks, mini-courses, curated resource packs
   - Premium: full courses, community access, coaching add-ons
3. **Validate:** check Gumroad competitors (pricing/reviews), search forums for recurring questions, pre-sell with a landing page.

### Step 2: Packaging & Pricing

4. **Product rules:** deliver clear transformation, include actionable frameworks/templates, add bonuses, professional PDF formatting.
5. **Pricing:** $9 cheat sheet, $19-29 eBook, $49-97 course. Use 3 tiers (Basic/Standard/Premium). Anchor high to make standard feel like a deal.
6. **Configure Gumroad:** benefit-driven title, 3-5 outcome bullets, cover image, variants with clear value.

### Step 3: Lead Magnet & Email Capture

7. **Create free lead magnet:** solves ONE specific problem, standalone value, natural upsell mention at end.
8. **Email capture:** Gumroad's built-in email requirement on free products, or embedded form (ConvertKit/Beehiiv). Thank-you page teases the paid product.

### Step 4: Sales Page Copy

9. **Structure:**
   ```
   HEADLINE: [Audience] + [Transformation] + [Timeframe]
   PROBLEM → AGITATE → SOLUTION
   FEATURES → BENEFITS (5-7 bullets)
   SOCIAL PROOF → BONUS → FAQ (3-5) → CTA → GUARANTEE
   ```

### Step 5: Email Funnel

10. **Nurture sequence (6 emails over 12 days):**
    - Day 0: Welcome, deliver lead magnet, share your story
    - Day 2: Teach one actionable lesson
    - Day 4: Expand on the problem, cost of inaction
    - Day 6: Soft pitch with testimonial
    - Day 8: Direct pitch with deadline/bonus
    - Day 12: Final call, urgency, objection handling

11. **Post-purchase sequence:**
    - Day 0: Delivery + getting-started guide
    - Day 3: Check-in, offer support
    - Day 7: Request testimonial/review
    - Day 14: Upsell to premium tier or next product

### Step 6: Launch & Metrics

12. **Launch checklist:** product uploaded, sales page live, email funnel active, test purchase verified, social posts scheduled, share in 2-3 communities.
13. **Weekly metrics:** subscriber→customer conversion (2-5%), email open rate (40%+ welcome, 25%+ nurture), revenue/subscriber, refund rate (<5%), top traffic sources.

---

## Constraints

- NEVER make false income claims or use manipulative urgency.
- NEVER gate essential info behind paywalls; paid product adds convenience, structure, depth.
- Lead magnet MUST deliver standalone value, not be a teaser.
- Max 3 emails/week during launch, 1/week during nurture.
- ALWAYS honor refund requests within stated policy.
- Pricing MUST be transparent. No hidden fees or mandatory upsells.
- Social proof MUST be genuine. Never fabricate testimonials.

## Examples

### Example 1: DevOps Notion Pack

```
Product: "The DevOps Command Center" Notion Templates
Price: $19 standard / $39 premium (with video walkthrough)
Lead Magnet: Free "Deployment Checklist" PDF
Funnel: 6-email sequence over 12 days
Audience: Junior-to-mid DevOps engineers
Hook: "Ship with confidence. Stop googling deployment steps."
Target: 4% conversion, $0.76/subscriber
```

### Example 2: Creator Email Course

```
Product: "Email That Converts" - 5-module video course
Price: $47 standard / $97 premium (+ 1:1 review)
Lead Magnet: Free "10 Subject Line Formulas" swipe file
Funnel: Launch-style, 48-hour open/close window
Audience: Newsletter creators (500-5K subscribers)
Hook: "Turn your 15% open rate into 45%. Here's the exact framework."
Upsell: "Subject Line Masterclass" ($29, post-purchase)
```
